Herminio Paucar

Live in São Paulo · Brazil · herminiopaucar@gmail.com

Currently, I am a doctoral student in Computer Science at Distributed Systems and Concurrent Programming Laboratory (LaSDPC) from Institute of Mathematics and Computer Science (ICMC) at São Paulo University (USP-Brazil).
I have a master's in CS from Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) and I am a software engineer from San Marcos University (UNMSM-Perú).
During my master's, I worked as a software developer on the V3o2 project at Institute of Technical-Scientific Software Development of PUC-Rio (TECGRAF).
I am a professor at the graduate and postgraduate levels and Technologie speaker at national and international events.

Topic of interests:

  • Software Architecture
  • Internet of Things
  • Grid, cloud and fog computing
  • Smart and Digital Farming
  • Artificial Intelligence
